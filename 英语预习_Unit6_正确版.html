📘 语法讲解

附加疑问句 (Question tags)

附加疑问句由"陈述句 + 简短问句"构成，用于确认或征求同意。

陈述句后的附加疑问句

肯定句否定附加疑问句
Xi'an is a good choice,isn't it?
We can see a lot of pandas there,can't we?
They will go by train,won't they?
Your sister wants to visit the Island of Music,doesn't she?
否定句肯定附加疑问句
He isn't going to Hainan this winter,is he?
You can't make the decision now,can you?
They won't go by plane,will they?
The bus doesn't stop here,does it?

祈使句后的附加疑问句

肯定句或否定句附加疑问句
Let's take the next bus,shall we?
Let's not go out tonight,shall we?
Return to your seats,will you?
Don't eat food on the bus,will you?

规则：
1. 肯定陈述 → 否定附加疑问句（e.g. There is a beach near our hotel, isn't there?）
2. 否定陈述 → 肯定附加疑问句（e.g. We can't go tomorrow, can we?）
3. 祈使句 → shall we（Let's）或 will you（其他）

be going to 表示将来

肯定
Iam going totravel around China.
She/Heis going to
You/We/Theyare going to
Itis going tobe an exciting trip.

我们用 be going to 来表达：
1. 将来的计划或安排（e.g. We are going to visit the science museum this afternoon.）
2. 意图、打算（e.g. I am going to exercise more often.）
3. 预测（e.g. It is not going to rain tomorrow.）

Note: Sometimes we say gonna (= going to) in casual, spoken English. 有时在非正式口语中用 gonna 代替 going to。
