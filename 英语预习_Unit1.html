📘 语法讲解 · 一般现在时 (Present Simple)

一般现在时 (Present Simple)

肯定句：

  • I/You/We/They like the school garden.
  • He/She/It likes the school garden.

否定句：

  • I/You/We/They don't like maths.
  • He/She/It doesn't like maths.

一般疑问句：

  • Do you run fast? — Yes, I do. / No, I don't.
  • Does he run fast? — Yes, he does. / No, he doesn't.

第三人称单数变化规则

规则变化示例
大多数动词+slike→likes, play→plays
以 s/x/sh/ch 结尾+eswash→washes, go→goes
辅音+y 结尾去 y +iesstudy→studies
不规则变形have→has, do→does
