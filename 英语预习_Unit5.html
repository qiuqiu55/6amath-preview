📘 语法讲解

疑问词 how、when、where、why

疑问词助动词主语动词其他
HowdoesUncle Linmilkcows?
Whendobatssleep?
Wheredoantsbuildtheir homes?
Whydohenspeckpeople?
疑问词be 动词主语其他
Howaredogsdifferent from cats?
WhenisWorld Animal Day?
Whereisthe horse?
Whyareanimalsimportant to us?

1. when 用于询问时间或日期。
e.g. When do they feed animals every day?

2. where 用于询问地点或方位。
e.g. Where are the goats?

3. why 用于询问原因。
e.g. Why do pigs roll in the mud?

4. how 用于询问事物发生的方式。
e.g. How do pigeons find their way home?

5. how + 形容词/副词 用于询问程度。
e.g. How far is the goat yard? How long can elephants live? How often do you walk your dog? How many goats does Uncle Lin have? How much milk does a cow produce in a day?
